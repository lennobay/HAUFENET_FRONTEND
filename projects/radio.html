Sommerfest Radio

Status: Ended Editet: 13.06.2026, 10:38

As part of our Sommerfestival at my school, the radio club also took part in it. The idea of the radio club was to create a livestream, like an real radio, where can interview diffrent persons at our festival. As such I was tasked to create a Webui, where customers could both stream radio and also send song wishes.

Livestream of Radio

In total I had three versions for this frontpage design. The first was a black design, the second a blue and the third an green-blue. The third was the last version as you can see in the picture aboth.

Songwish

The songwish was created the evening before the festival, meaning it wasn't so well designed and it could be definetly upgraded.

Behind it was a API written in Golang and the DB redis. Both where hosted on the VPS. The version you see, which was also the last one, lacked important features. Such as an indicater for a succesfull send of the wish or any error message. Included was also a interfaces where the technitians could also see wished and delete them.
